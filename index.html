We are a leading Laboratory in Calibration, Dimensional Measurement and Testing services for controlled environments.

We also distribute measuring instruments from recognized brands.

We have six branches in the Mexican Republic, with headquarters located in Tijuana, Baja California and branches in Mexicali, Baja California; Rosarito, Baja California; Ensenada, Baja California; Tecate, Baja California.

Our laboratories have certificationISO/IEC 17025:2017 and the national standard ANSI/NCSL Z540-1-1994 (R2002) by the ANSI-ASQ National Accreditation Board (ANAB) and meets the requirements of international standards.
