Returns, Exchanges, and Cancellations Policy for CALIRISK

CALIRISK has a policy for returns, exchanges, and cancellations on products purchased from the official CALIRISK Online Store at www.caliriskdemexico.com/store.

Requesting Returns or Exchanges

You can request a return or exchange for a product purchased at CALIRISK only under two circumstances: if the product has visible damage or if the product received is different from the one you ordered.

When the Product has Visible Damage:

  • Email us at info@calirisk.us with the subject line “Return Request for Damage” and your order number.
  • In the email, please provide:
    • • Your order number
    • • The model of the purchased product
    • • The brand of the purchased product
    • • Attach your order confirmation email
    • Include photos of:
      • • The label and box in which you received the package
      • • The product box
      • • The product showing the visible damage
  • Note that:
    • • The product must be in its original packaging, sealed, and in perfect condition.
    • • If it is part of a package or includes a gift, everything must be returned complete: packaging, manuals, and original accessories.

The maximum time to receive your "Return Request for Damage" is 7 days from the date of receipt. Shipping costs are non-refundable. Requests submitted after 7 days from receipt are not eligible for returns.

Conditions for Non-Returnable Products:

  • • The product has been used.
  • • The packaging is open, scratched, damaged, dirty, wet, or otherwise tampered with.
  • • The product was a special order.
  • • The product is obsolete or discontinued.
  • • The product description includes “No Returns” or similar language.
  • • The product was sold as clearance.

If your return request is approved, you will receive an email confirmation along with a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA #) and instructions to ship the product back. You must ship the product properly packed and with paid postage. The customer is responsible for insurance, taxes, and any other legal or logistical concerns during the return process.

When the Product Received is Incorrect:

If the product received does not match the model and brand you ordered, you have up to 24 hours from receipt to notify us. Please call (664) 387-5607 or email us at info@calirisk.us with the subject line “Return Request for Incorrect Model” and your order number.

  • In the email, please provide:
    • • Your order number
    • • The model and brand of the purchased product
    • • The quantity (if multiple items of the same model)
    • • Attach your order confirmation email
    • • Include photos of:
      • • The label and box where you received the package
      • • The product box, front and back
  • If approved, you will receive an RMA # and a prepaid shipping label to attach to the package for pickup by the courier.

If the correct product is unavailable, a credit note for the product amount and shipping fees will be issued, which you can use for a future purchase or request a refund.

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel an order only if it has not been shipped. If payment has been made but the order has not been shipped, request cancellation by calling (664) 387-5607 or emailing store@calirisk.us with the subject line “Order Cancellation #xxxxxx.” Please include a brief description of the reason for cancellation.

Refund Policy

  • • A refund is only eligible if the cancellation is within the time specified in the Cancellation Policy.
  • • A refund is only eligible when a return is approved (see Return Policy).
  • • A refund is only eligible if the product is out of stock after notifying you (see Order Management Intervention).

Customer service is available Monday through Friday from 8 AM to 5 PM Pacific Time.
